I am mostly about a good adventure. I’m not very picky. I don’t plan too much. And a lot of things “go wrong”... a lot. But I think the best adventures are not expensive and not super well planned (most of the best are accidental) and on the verge of a little dumb. I prefer nature to big busy cities but my favorite are cities surrounded by natural beauty. I LOVE a good hostel. I like cooking my own meals to save money. I am a big foodie but not a big sit-down restaurant guy... street food is better! I love walking tours. I love running/biking through a city. I prefer the more-hole-in-the-Wall places to the big name places. I prefer the off-the-beaten-path cities. I like the districts of towns with the weird haircuts, the beanies, the cigarettes smokers and the musicians. The tattoos and the piercings. I’m not a huge partier but enjoy staying out till 5am with someone I just met that day from another country. If any of this sounds like you, then you might find some of this slightly useful. But if your anything like me, you probably will take it all with a healthy bit if skepticism and think you can figure it out when you get there — which you don’t need me to tell you, you totally can.
